# Types

## AttendanceState

Represents one of the states a student can be when a teacher takes attendance.

**Definition:**
`"present"` | `"late"` | `"absent"` | `"excused"` | `"left early"`

## GradeLevel

The grade level of a course.

**Definition:**
`"小1"` | `"小2"` | `"小3"` | `"小4"` | `"小5"` | `"小6"` | `"中1"` | `"中2"` | `"中3"` | `"高1"` | `"高2"` | `"高3"`
